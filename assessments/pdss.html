Panic Disorder Severity Scale
PDSS-SR · 7 items · Self-report
Several questions refer to panic attacks and limited symptom attacks. A panic attack is a sudden rush of fear or discomfort with at least 4 of the listed symptoms, peaking within 10 minutes. Episodes with fewer than 4 symptoms are limited symptom attacks. Please answer based on the past week.
Question 1
How many panic and limited symptom attacks did you have during the week?
Question 2
If you had any panic attacks during the past week, how distressing were they while they were happening? (If none, answer for limited symptom attacks.)
Question 3
During the past week, how much have you worried or felt anxious about when your next panic attack would occur or about fears related to the attacks?
Question 4
During the past week, were there any places or situations you avoided, or felt afraid of, because of fear of having a panic attack? Rate your level of fear and avoidance.
Question 5
During the past week, were there any activities you avoided, or felt afraid of, because they caused physical sensations like those during panic attacks, or might trigger one? Rate your level of fear and avoidance.
Question 6
During the past week, how much did the above symptoms altogether interfere with your ability to work or carry out responsibilities at home?
Question 7
During the past week, how much did panic and limited symptom attacks, worry about attacks, and fear of situations/activities interfere with your social life?
